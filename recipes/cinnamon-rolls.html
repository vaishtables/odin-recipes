Cinnamon Rolls

This is a recipe for cinnamon rolls.

I don't know what else to say. I like this recipe because it's eggless.

Ingredients

Tangzhong

  • 113g milk
  • 3 tbsp bread flour

Dough

  • 151g milk
  • 300g bread flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tsp instant yeast
  • 57g softened butter

Filling

  • 14g melted butter
  • 107g brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp bread flour
  • 4 tsp cinnamon
  • pinch of salt
